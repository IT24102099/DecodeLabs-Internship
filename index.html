How CivicFix Works

Reporting a community issue is simple. Follow these four steps to help improve your city.

1

Report an Issue

Submit the issue with details, photos and location.

2

Department Review

The issue is automatically assigned to the correct department.

3

Officer Assigned

Officers review the issue and begin the required work.

4

Issue Resolved

Citizens receive updates when the issue has been completed.
